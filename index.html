Aspect Ratio Modes

The right mode for every video

Switch modes in one click or cycle through with Alt+C. Each mode is tuned for a specific scenario.

Off

Original — Black Bars Visible

No transform applied. The video plays at its native size with black bars on ultrawide screens.

21:9 Crop

Crop to Cinematic 21:9

Crops the video to a 21:9 widescreen ratio — ideal for movies shot in cinematic scope.

Stretch Fill

Stretch to Fill Screen

Horizontally stretches the video to cover your full ultrawide display with no black bars.

Auto

Auto Force Aspect Ratio

Reads the video's native ratio and scales it intelligently to fill your ultrawide monitor.
